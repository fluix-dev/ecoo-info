Welcome to the 2021 ECOO programming contest!

This year, the contest will run as a single, individual, online contest for high school students. This contest aims to provide computer science students an opportunity to demonstrate their skills and knowledge while representing themselves and their schools against other computer science students in the province.

Key Details

Team Size: Individual
Registration Deadline: April 14th 6PM EDT
Practice Contest: April 15-20, 2021
Main Contest: May 1st, 2021 — 11:00 AM to 2:00 PM EDT
Problem Format: 6 problems — difficulty from CCC Junior Problem 1 to IOI

Registration

To register, please either use the Registration Form or through your teacher if they're collecting bulk registrations for your school.

Prizes

Whether ECOO-CS will provide prizes is yet to be decided; however, contest results will be publicly available so individual schools or boards may provide prizes on their own.

Contact

For any comments, questions, or concerns, please email ecoo@mcpt.ca.
