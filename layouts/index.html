Congratulations on the 2021 ECOO Programming Contest!

Congratulations to everyone who participated in the 2021 ECOO Programming Contest! This year we had 692 registered students, 473 of which competed in the contest. The top three students are as follows:

1. Zixiang Zhou from London Central Secondary School
2. (Tie) Edward Xiao from University of Toronto Schools
2. (Tie) Andrew Dong from Centennial Collegiate and Vocational Institute

Results

A complete scoreboard for the contest is available here.

Leaderboards for individual boards containing students' real names have been emailed to the appropriate contacts within them. Certificates of participation, certificates of distinction, and monetary prizes have been sent out.
